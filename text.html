Hello everyone,  
 
Last year, we launched the Global Inclusion and Diversity (I&D) Awards to celebrate our people and the many ways you advance inclusion, diversity, and well-being at Avanade. The response was incredible: more than 300 submissions from across the world!  

 

With submissions now open for this year’s awards, please nominate a colleague whose I&D work deserves special recognition. The 17 award categories demonstrate how deeply our people embed I&D principles in everything they do, from developing innovative technology solutions that advance well-being to participating in an Employee Network to cultivating a sense of belonging on their team. This year’s ceremony will feature an enhanced focus on well-being, including the new Courageous Storyteller Award, designed to spotlight a well-being role model who uses their personal story to beat stigma and inspire those around them.  

 

Amid ongoing legislation to limit rights and derail diversity efforts in the U.S., a global increase in anti-LGBTQ+ rhetoric and other challenging developments worldwide, now is an important time to reaffirm our commitment to fostering an inclusive culture. These awards offer the opportunity to pause and reflect on how we all succeed in a more inclusive world: one that celebrates the value of difference, and where everyone knows they can reach their full potential by embracing who they are and what makes them unique. Take a moment to consider the many role models around you who make a genuine human impact by shaping this vision and please nominate them for an award. We can’t wait to read their stories.  

 

The deadline for nominations is September 8th. You’ll find a link to nominations here, with more information about individual awards and eligibility available on the I&D SharePoint page.
